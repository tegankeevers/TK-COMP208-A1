About Me

Welcome to my Professional Practice ePortfolio! My name is Tegan Keevers, and I am a pre-service teacher studying at ACU. On the website, I will share my experiences and insights into the teaching and education field. As a pre-service teacher, I have accumulated a wealth of knowledge and advice that I am excited to share and evaluate on this page. My ePortfolio highlights my teaching philosophy, explores relevant teaching artefacts and revised lessons plans used to support my practice. Here, I will showcase my skills and achievements, and provide evidence of my professional growth and development.

I am a woman working in a teaching discipline that is predominantly lead by older male teachers, I am striving to change that narrative, proving that gender does not decide which technology subject you have to teacher. I aim to encourage gender equality throughout the key learning area of TAS, ensuring that all students have equal opportunity to complete the subjects they wish.
